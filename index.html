How to Use:
  1. Position your camera to capture your face clearly
  2. Ensure adequate lighting for best accuracy
  3. The system will immediately start monitoring
  4. Red alerts = Drowsiness detected (eyes closed)
  5. Yellow alerts = Yawning detected
  6. If drowsy for 15+ seconds, SMS alert will be sent (if configured)
  7. Keep the browser window open for continuous monitoring
Configuration:

To enable SMS alerts:

  1. Sign up at Twilio.com
  2. Get your Account SID and Auth Token
  3. Edit app.py and fill in the credentials
  4. Configure recipient phone number (must be verified)
  5. Restart the application

Note: Requires valid Twilio account credentials
